# 📚 ANOTHER SKY - Universe Documentation

## 🌌 Project Overview

**Another Sky** is a transmedia horror mystery universe spanning three interconnected apocalyptic narratives across multiple realities. This repository contains the complete lore bible, world-building documentation, and story structure for a connected universe exploring the collapse of reality through biological, supernatural, and cosmic horror.

---

## 📖 The Three Stories

### 1️⃣ **SIMULUNAS** (Entry Point - Shortest)
**Genre:** Post-Apocalyptic Sci-Fi  
**Year:** 2048  
**Format:** Short story (few chapters)

Earth cracks open after a geological event called "The Great Thinning," releasing a mysterious yellow gas that melts anything containing H²O. All biological life dies within days. Only androids running on Hydrocuelaris cell batteries survive in a world devoid of humanity.

**Protagonist:** Japanese environmental engineer + EVA-5 android  
**Themes:** Loneliness, purpose without humans, final moments of biological life

---

### 2️⃣ **THE BEYONDERS** (Second Entry - Medium Length)
**Genre:** Superhuman/Powers Fiction  
**Year:** 2032 (World -1)  
**Format:** Multi-POV novel (4 perspectives, 1 main)

A meteor named Tartarus-2628ABH approaches Earth. Instead of destroying it, the meteor fragments into sapphire blue sparkles that grant every living being impossible powers. From ants hurling 3-ton rocks to humans controlling fundamental forces, reality becomes chaos.

**Key Mystery:** Where is Som? Someone has taken his place.  
**Structure:** 4 POV characters navigating powered apocalypse  
**Themes:** Power corruption, identity, the cost of ascension

---

### 3️⃣ **ANOTHER SKY** (Anchor Story - Longest)
**Genre:** Zombie Horror Mystery / Reality Horror  
**Year:** 2032 (World -2)  
**Format:** Full novel + pixel RPG game adaptation (Godot)

Som, a 28-year-old civil engineer in Dhaka, Bangladesh, wakes up in a reality that isn't his own—though he doesn't realize it yet. As a zombie outbreak consumes the world, he discovers nine people he loved have been completely erased from existence. When his mother becomes the first "Ghuul"—a reality-glitching monstrosity—Som's quest for truth becomes a journey between worlds.

**Protagonist:** Som (সোম - "Moon" in Sanskrit)  
**Core Mystery:** Why was he swapped? Where is Anne? What is he becoming?  
**Themes:** Memory, identity, grief for the erased, reality displacement

## 🔗 How They Connect

### **The Yellow Gas**
- Appears in **Simulunas (2048)** as global extinction event
- Appears in **Another Sky (2032)** as "Fog of Medusa" in Germany
- **Same substance bleeding between worlds/timelines**

### **The Three Apocalypses**
- **World -1 (Beyonders):** Biology AMPLIFIED → Everyone gains powers
- **World -2 (Another Sky):** Biology CORRUPTED → Zombies, infected, Ghuuls
- **World -?? (Simulunas):** Biology ELIMINATED → Yellow gas kills all organic life

**Pattern:** Enhance → Corrupt → Eliminate

### **Character Connections**
- **Som:** Vanishes from World -1 on March 31, 2032 → Transferred to World -2
- **Anne & Som's Mother:** Exist in World -1 (cameo backgrounds in Beyonders) but are **completely erased** from World -2
- **The Replacement:** Someone/something took Som's place in World -1

### **Timeline Synchronization**
```
March 31, 2032  → Som transfers from World -1 to World -2
April 7, 2032   → Tartarus detected approaching World -1
April 5, 2032   → Zombie outbreak begins in World -2
July 1, 2032    → Tartarus hits World -1 (3 months after Som vanishes)
July 2032       → Som 3 months into apocalypse in World -2, unaware World -1 became superhuman
2048           → Yellow gas erupts globally (Simulunas)
```

## 🌍 World Structure

### **World -1 (Original Earth)**
- Som's home world
- Normal timeline until March 31, 2032
- Tartarus meteor event July 1, 2032
- Everyone gains powers
- Anne and Som's mother exist here

### **World -2 (Another Sky)**
- Altered timeline (divergence after 1946)
- WW3 occurred (2025-2027, 672M deaths)
- Cygnus signal received 2007: *"They lied to us. Send help."*
- Moon Dome built 2012 by cleaning product company
- Zombie outbreak April 2032
- Anne and 8 others never existed here
- Yellow gas leaks appear in Germany

### **World -?? (Simulunas)**
- Future Earth 2048 OR separate reality
- Great Thinning geological event
- Yellow gas global eruption
- Complete biological extinction
- Only androids remain

## 👥 Key Characters

### **Som (সোম)**
- Age: 28 (born 2003)
- Profession: Civil Engineer, Japanese firm in Dhaka
- Appearance: 5'11", 72kg, mesomorph, greying hair (trauma), glasses
- Personality: Confident at home, awkward at work, jack-of-all-trades
- Languages: Bangla, English, basic Japanese
- **Status:** Transferred to World -2, can never return, becomes "something else"

### **Som's Mother**
- Age: 68, Hindu, BPD survivor
- History: Survived 1971 independence war, Bhola cyclone, famines, WW3
- Physical: Weak legs, paralyzed fingers, constant pain
- **World -1:** Alive, has powers (cameo in Beyonders)
- **World -2:** Becomes first Ghuul (Incident Zero, ~April 19)

### **Anne**
- Filipina artist/dancer/singer, political activist
- 6-year long-distance relationship with Som
- Broke up to focus on career but still loves him
- **World -1:** Exists, has powers (cameo in Beyonders)
- **World -2:** NEVER EXISTED - completely erased
- **Som's tragedy:** Mourns someone who doesn't exist in his world

## 🧟 Entity Types (Another Sky - World -2)

### **Zombies (9 Types)**
1. **Skoth** - Original outbreak type, decayed after 9 months
2. **Glowbubs** - Attracted to light, stare until it goes out
3. **Jawies** - Can crush metal with jaws, muscular humans
4. **Whites** - White-haired females, vibration-sensitive, calcified claws
5. **Oldbones** - Elderly with sharp spinal protrusions
6. **Disabled** - Blind (enhanced hearing) / Deaf (enhanced sight) / Crippled (mutated limbs)
7. **Noire** - Nocturnal ambush hunters, slight hive mind
8. **Bloaters** - Scream to summon hordes
9. **Aquatic** - Rare underwater type

### **Infected (6 Types - WIP Names)**
Retain 6-8 year-old intelligence, avoid familiar humans, attack strangers, eyes reflect light

1. **Grübler** (German) - "They think" - Strategic intelligence
2. **Mówcy** (Polish) - "They talk" - Mimic voices, blend in
3. **Klamáři** (Czech) - "They trick" - Skinwalker-like, fake loved ones
4. **Byggare** (Swedish) - "They build" - Construct unknown structures
5. **[TBD Japanese]** - "Glitch" - Flicker between realities, extremely dangerous
6. **[TBD]** - "They take" - Kidnap and consume humans alive

### **Ghuuls**
- Created when bitten by BOTH zombie AND infected (extremely rare)
- Pure white body/hair, eyes flicker brown/black
- Glitch between realities
- Strongest threat, nearly unkillable
- 273 known worldwide
- **First Ghuul:** Som's mother

## 🌧️ Anomalies

### **Rain of Obseida (Another Sky)**
- Deep black rain, starts 2 weeks post-outbreak
- Slightly acidic but harmless to humans
- Makes infected calm/still
- Sky appears to "bleed" during rainfall
- Daily occurrence after initial appearances

### **Fog of Medusa (Another Sky)**
- Yellow gas appearing in Germany
- Kills everything EXCEPT zombies
- Even infected die in the fog
- **Same gas as Simulunas yellow gas**
- Reality bleed from future/other world

### **The Great Thinning (Simulunas)**
- Geological event causing Earth to crack
- Yellow gas erupts from core globally
- Melts anything with H²O
- Complete biological extinction within days

## 🔬 Technology

### **Hydrocuelaris Cell Battery**
- Invented by thermology engineering student
- Original goal: Flask staying hot for 1 month without temp drop
- **Hybrid:** Mechanical + bacterial components
- Made electricity 24% cheaper by 2046
- Takes time to build
- Powers androids in Simulunas
- **Possible connection:** Response to yellow gas threat?

### **Moon Dome**
- Built 2012 by cleaning product company
- Located on the Moon
- Elite/government evacuation during outbreak
- Status unknown
- **Suspicious timing:** Built 20 years before Tartarus

## 🎭 Major Factions (Another Sky)

### **The Remaining Governments**
- Alaska safe zone (USA, UK, Canada, India, France, Germany, S. Korea, Finland)
- Possibly planning nuclear "restart"
- Claims to be "humanity's last hope"

### **The Moon Dwellers**
- Billionaires/politicians who fled to Moon Dome
- Status unknown
- Theories: Dead via cannibalism OR terraforming Moon

### **The Blood Pact**
- Criminal syndicate, Antarctica-based
- Organ smuggling operation
- Started as Mexican biker gang

### **Rooftop Seers**
- Radio broadcasters spreading "God's word"
- Secretly infected

### **Logbook Drifters**
- Unknown numbers worldwide
- Distribute handwritten logbooks with zombie/infected intel
- Logo: Owl holding down lizard
- Counter-faction spreading fake logbooks

- Mourning what no one else remembers

- Som becomes "something else"
